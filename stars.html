Stars are massive objects that are luminous and very hot balls of hot gas (plasma). Many of them are visible with a naked eye at night and one (Sun) is also visible during the day. Though stars appear small from Earth, they are incredibly massive. Out Sun, which is considered a relatively small star, is able to fit 1,300,000 Earths!
