Galaxies are one of the most beautiful objects in the universe. But, what are galaxies exactly? Well, if we think about each star as a person, then galaxies are giant cities where stars live. The sizes of glaxies can be truly mind-boggling. Our own galaxy alone (Milky Way) contains close to 1 trillion stars. 1 trillion is a number that our brains simply cannot imagine or visualize.

There are, however, many billions of galaxies in the universe, each containing billions and billions of stars. The scale of all these things is just unimaginably big.
